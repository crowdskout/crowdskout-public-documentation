Address

Region

People who live in a specific region.

Criterion Region

This takes values dependent on the data available to you, but will contain all the states you have individuals.

Filters

  • County - Optional - People who live in a specific county within the region, it will conflict with the City filter if both are applied
  • City - Optional - People who live in a specific city within the region, it will conflict with the County filter if both are applied

Examples

People who live in Illinois:

(Region = "Illinois")

City

People who live in a specific city.

Criterion City

This allows values dependent on the data available to you.

Filters

  • Region - Required - The region that the city we're looking for lies in

Examples

People who live in Chicago, Illinois:

(City = "Chicago", Region = "Illinois")

Postal Code

People who live in a specific postal code.

Criterion PostalCode

Allows either a 5 digit zip code, or a 5 digit zip code plus 4 more digits separated by a dash.

Examples

People who live in 60637:

(PostalCode = "60637")

People who live in 60637-1756:

(PostalCode = "60637-1756")

County

People who live in a specific county.

Criterion County

The values this can take depend on your available data.

Filters

  • Region - Required - The state this county belongs in

Examples

People who live in Cook County, Illinois:

(County = "Cook", Region = "Illinois")

Geographical Polygon

Allows you to find people within a polygon on a map.

Criterion Polygon

This takes a GeoJSON encoded polygon string.

Examples

People who live within the polygon bounded by the latitude, and longitude points [0, 0], [80, 0], [80, 40], [0, 40]:

(Polygon = "[[[0,0], [80,0], [80,40], [0,40]]]")
