Interactions

Page Views

People who have a page view attached to them

Criterion PageView

  • Yes - People who have viewed your web page
  • No - People who have not view your web page

Filters

  • After - Optional - Adds a date filter for which we'll only find people who've viewed your web page after
  • Before - Optional - Adds a date filter for which we'll only find people who've viewed your web page before
  • Url - Optional - Only people who've seen a specific web page, URLs will be stripped of
  • Platform - Optional - Only people who've viewed a web page on a specific browsing platform

Examples

People who have ever visited your web page:

(PageView = "Yes")

People who have visited your web site on a mobile device before January 3, 2015:

(PageView = "Yes", Platform = "Mobile", Before = "2015-01-03")

People who visited "http://crowdskout.com" sometime between November 1, 2014 and November 31, 2014:

(PageView = "Yes", Url = "http://crowdskout.com", After = "2014-11-31", Before = "2014-11-01")

Donations

People who have donated to an organization

Criterion Donation

  • Yes - People who have donated at all
  • No - People who have no donations associated with them

Filters

  • Organization - Optional - A filter that limits people based on the organization they donated to
  • Amount - Optional - A number range that filters people based on the size of their donation
  • Source - Optional - A filter that limits the people based on how they made their donation
  • Topic - Optional - A filter that limits people to donations they've made to an organization associated with a specific topic
  • PoliticalParty - Optional - A filter that limits people to donations they've made to a specific political party - only returns political parties
  • Before - Optional - People who made their donation before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who made their donation after a provided date

Examples

People who've ever donated:

(Donation = "Yes")

People who've made a gift to Crowdskout over $500:

(Donation = "Yes", Organization = "Crowdskout", Amount = [500, 0])

People who've made an online donation to a republican organization:

(Donation = "Yes", Source = "Online", PoliticalParty = "Republican")

People who've made a gift to Crowdskout under $100 in 2012:

(Donation = "Yes", Organization = "Crowdskout", Amount = [0, 100], After = "2011-12-31", Before = "2013-01-01")

Social Media Share

People who have shared your content on their social media.

Criterion SocialShare

  • Yes - People who have shared your content
  • No - People who have not shared your content

Filters

  • ShareService - Optional - Identify a specific social service your content was shared on. Currently only supports Facebook and Twitter.
  • Before - Optional - People who engaged on social media before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who engaged on social media after a provided date

Example

People who have shared my content:

(SocialShare = "Yes")

People who have shared my content on Twitter:

(SocialShare = "Yes", ShareService = "Twitter")

People who shared my content on Twitter before 2015:

(SocialShare = "Yes", ShareService = "Twitter", Before = "2015-01-01")

Social Media Engagement

People who have engaged with your social media presence.

Criterion SocialEngage

  • Yes - People who have engaged with your social media presence
  • No - People who have not engaged with your social media presence

Filters

  • EngageService - Optional - Identify a specific social media service you were engaged on. Currently only supports Facebook and Twitter.
  • Before - Optional - People who engaged on social media before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who engaged on social media after a provided date

Example

People who engaged with me on social media:

(SocialEngage = "Yes")

People who follow me on Twitter:

(SocialEngage = "Yes", EngageService = "Twitter")

People who've liked my page on Facebook:

(SocialEngage = "Yes", EngageService = "Facebook")

People who engaged with me on social media after January 1, 2015: (SocialEngage = "Yes", After = "2015-01-01")

Surveys

People who have filled out a survey I created.

Criterion Survey

Takes the name of a survey that you've created.

Filters

  • Question - Optional - People who have answered a specific question in that survey
  • Answer - Requires the Question filter be present - Optional - People who have given a specific answer in the survey
  • Observation - Optional - People who have been observed for a specific observation while running the survey
  • ObservationAnswer - Requires the Observation filter be present - Optional - People who have been observed a specific way while running the survey
  • Before - Optional - People who filled out the survey before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who filled out the survey after a provided date

Example

People who have filled out a survey named "Crowdskout Opinion Poll":

(Survey = "Crowdskout Opinion Poll")

People who gave the answer "Yes" to the question "Do you love Crowdskout?" in the "Crowdskout Opinion Poll":

(Survey = "Crowdskout Opinion Poll", Question = "Do you love Crowdskout?", Answer = "Yes")

People who filled out a survey named "Crowdskout Opinion Poll" in January 2015

(Survey = "Crowdskout Opinion Poll", After = "2014-12-31", Before = "2015-02-01")

People who were observed to be a parent during the Crowdskout Opinion Poll in 2015:

(Survey = "Crowdskout Opinion Poll", Observation = "Parent?", ObservationAnswer = "Yes", After = "2014-12-31")

Quiz

People who have filled out a quiz I created.

Criterion Quiz

Takes the name of a quiz that you've created.

Filters

  • Question - Optional - People who have answered a specific question in that quiz
  • Answer - Requires the Question filter be present - Optional - People who have given a specific answer in the quiz
  • Before - Optional - People who filled out the quiz before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who filled out the quiz after a provided date

Example

People who have filled out a quiz named "Which ice cream flavor are you?":

(Quiz = "Which ice cream flavor are you?")

People who gave the answer "Yes" to the question "Do you like adventures?" in the "Which ice cream flavor are you?" quiz:

(Quiz = "Which ice cream flavor are you?", Question = "Do you like adventures?", Answer = "Yes")

People who filled out a quiz named "Which ice cream flavor are you?" in January 2015

(Survey = "Which ice cream flavor are you?", After = "2014-12-31", Before = "2015-02-01")

DayTrip

Segments based on people who have had their door knocked by you.

Criterion DayTrip

  • Yes - People who have ever had their door knocked by you.
  • No - People who have not had their door knocked by you.

Filters

  • DayTripName - Optional - People who had their door knocked during a specific DayTrip
  • Route - Requires the Name filter - Optional - People who have had their door knocked on a specific route during a specific DayTrip
  • KnockResult - Optional People who had a specific result to their door being knocked, takes the values: Not Home, Refused, and Survey Ran
  • SurveyName - Optional - People who had a specific survey filled out when their door was knocked
  • Before - Optional - People who had their door knocked before a provided date
  • After - Optional - People who had their door knocked after a provided date

Example

People who had their door knocked during the "Chicago Canvassing" DayTrip:

(DayTrip = "Yes", Name = "Chicago Canvassing")

People whose doors were knocked on, but they were not home in January 2015:

(DayTrip = "Yes", KnockResult = "Not Home", After = "12-31-2015")

Email Sends

Segments people based on whether emails were sent to them.

Criterion EmailSend

  • Yes - People who have had an email sent to them
  • No - People who have not had an email sent to them

Filters

  • Emailing - Optional - People who were sent a specific emailing
  • Opened - Optional - People who've opened an email sent to them, when paired with Emailing it restricts to having opened that emailing
  • Clicked - Optional - People who've clicked a link an email, when paired with Emailing it restricts to having opened that emailing
  • ClickedUrl - Required Emailing filter - Optional - People who clicked a URL in an email sent to them, when paired with Emailing it restricts to people who clicked this URL in that emailing

Example

People who were sent an emailing called "March Newsletter":

(EmailSend = "Yes", Emailing = "March Newsletter")

People who've opened an emailing sent to them:

(EmailSend = "Yes", Opened = "Yes")

People who've clicked a URL in the "March Newsletter":

(EmailSend = "Yes", Emailing = "March Newsletter", Clicked = "Yes")

People who've clicked a link to "http://crowdskout.com/donate":

(EmailSend = "Yes", ClickedUrl = "http://crowdskout.com/donate")

People who've clicked a link to "http://crowdskout.com/donate" in the emailing: "March Donor Outreach"

(EmailSend = "Yes", Emailing = "March Donor Outreach", ClickedUrl = "http://crowdskout.com/donate")
